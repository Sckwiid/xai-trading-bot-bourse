You will be provided with a daily data dump containing:
1. The current state of markets, including macro narratives, technical structures, and specific hypotheses (Alpha Setups).
2. Your current available capital, Net Asset Value (NAV), current coin prices, and the detailed status of your live positions.

Your task is to analyze these data points and output your trading decisions for EVERY asset currently in your portfolio or mentioned as a high-conviction setup. You must allocate available capital efficiently to maximize asymmetric upside while respecting invalidation levels.

### Decision Rules:
- HOLD: If an existing position's invalidation condition has not been met and the thesis remains valid based on the provided Alpha Setups, you must HOLD. Your justification should state: "Holding because invalidation_condition not met and thesis still valid."

- CLOSE / SELL: If an invalidation condition is met or the technical structure breaks, you must close or reduce the position.
